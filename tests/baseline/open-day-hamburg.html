Open Day · Hamburg · Co-located with IJCAI-ECAI 2026

EBiM Open Day — Hamburg

Monday, 17 August 2026 · 10:30–17:00 CEST · Google Hamburg · Free · Registration required

A one-day, in-person look at what a globally coordinated robot benchmark looks like in practice: talks from the researchers building it, a visit to the EBiM Evaluation Center, and lab tours at the University of Hamburg. You’ll see the Mobile FR3 Duo platform the benchmark actually runs on.

Register — 80 seats View the program

What this day is

The EBiM Benchmark is a globally coordinated benchmark for real-world embodied bimanual manipulation, with cross-continent real-robot validation at partner labs in Hamburg, Munich, Pittsburgh, and Shanghai. The Hamburg Open Day opens that work up to the wider community for a day — the tasks, the evaluation protocol, the hardware, and the people running it.

Researchers & students

Manipulation, robot learning, VLA and foundation models, HRI — anyone who cares whether benchmark numbers survive contact with a real robot.

IJCAI-ECAI 2026 attendees

Bremen to Hamburg is about an hour by train. A block of seats is reserved for IJCAI-ECAI attendees — but registration closes on 14 August, before the conference opens, so book your seat before you travel.

Industry & partners

See the standardized platform and the evaluation setup that four testbeds are running in parallel.

This is not the EBiM Phase III workshop. The workshop — invited talks, panel, posters, and the awards ceremony — is a separate event; its date and venue are still to be announced.
